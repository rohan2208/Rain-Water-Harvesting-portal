EASY INSTALLATION

Stage 1: Rainwater that collects on the terrace makes its way down a pipe.

Stage 2: The suction tank filters the water and then sends it to the collection tank for storage.

Stage 3: from the collection tank, the water is pumped to the rooftop tank.
